FitNotes Documentation - iOS
-----

Welcome to the FitNotes Documentation. 

Here you will find resources to help you get the most out of FitNotes and your workouts.

If you have any questions or suggestions please email us at [FitNotes@outlook.com](mailto:FitNotes@outlook.com)

# v2.9.0 Release:
NEW: Personal Records! We have added the notion of personal records and back filled that data. 
Any time you hit a new PR the Set icon will be displayed in gold. 
There is also a new option in the settings tab to continue your workouts from your last personal record.

NEW: Body Measurements! You can now create and record different body measurements. 
You will find the body measurement definition in the settings tab, we have created a few to get your started. 
To record a body measurement look inside your workouts, at the very top section, 
you will find a new button that will lead you to a new screen. 
You can also track your measurements with a new chart in the Explore tab.

NEW: Improvements to the timer! During an active workout you can now bring up a timer on demand. 
You will find a new button on the top right. Also, when the timer is going, you can click it to edit the time remaining.

NEW: Hide completed sets. During an active workout you will find a new button to the left of the "Finish Workout" button. 
Clicking the button will hide completed sets from your view, sort of a To-Do mode.

NEW: You can now mark a set as "Warm Up", which will exclude it from various calculations. 
Simply keep clicking on the row button until you hit it (it comes after the failed state), it will be marked with a 'W'.

NEW: Custom date range in Explore tab. The date filter was moved to the top section, away from the chart, and now allows 
selecting custom date ranges.

NEW: Explore button has been added to workouts in the Logs tab.

NEW: You can now copy a workout. Simply swipe left and tap the copy button.

NEW: Search bars have been added to the Workout and Logs tab. They allow searching by almost anything, give it a try!

NEW: Exercise template can now be shared. The share button for both workouts and exercises can be found when viewing the item details.

FIXED: Links in notes should be clickable now.

FIXED: Explore section had performance updates done to it and should load much faster for users with a lot of data.

FIXED: Clicking explore while viewing a workout will take you to the Historical Set Detail chart.

FIXED: The move up/down arrow button functionality, that is found while viewing an exercise template in a workout, has been fixed and should not crash anymore.

FIXED: Various UI bugs and improvements.
